Discover Luxury and Nature at Mahindra Codename Green Life: 50+ Premium Amenities for Modern Living

Mahindra Codename Green Life offers an elevated lifestyle with state-of-the-art amenities. Located at Kalyan-Bhiwandi Bypass, this project blends luxury and comfort across 10 acres. It boasts over 50+ amenities including a modern swimming pool, a fully-equipped gym, tennis courts, landscaped gardens, and more. The 14,000 sq. ft. clubhouse acts as a social hub for residents, while the peaceful green spaces encourage relaxation. With prices starting at ₹40.99 lakhs for 1BHK and ₹60.99 lakhs for 2BHK, Mahindra Green Life offers luxurious yet affordable living with proximity to key infrastructure projects like Metro Line 5 and Samruddhi Mahamarg Highway.
